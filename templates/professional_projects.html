Amber Lendability Model

Domian : Finance

Objective was to calculate the credit worthiness of customers who are new to credit using their demographics and sms / bank- statement data. This score was provided to CreditMantri’s partner lender to along with the expected delinquency rate to help them to decide whether they want to lend to the customer or not.

Impactability Model

Domian : Healthcare

Estimation of the member’s likelihood to preventable inpatient hospitalization, Emergency room visits and readmission before and after closure of the existing care- gaps. The model is developed to provide cost-effectiveness references for preventive care-management programs by estimate the magnitude and significance of the effects of care-gaps factors and estimate saving by closing and/or medication gaps should these apply and calculating the proportion of impactable population.

Risk Score Model

Domian : Healthcare

This risk score evaluates how likely and severe the risk is in the concurrent year, and what measures should be in place to effectively prevent or control the harm from happening. It can be used to by health plans for underwriting, identifying high cost patients for disease management programs, and measuring provider efficiency.
